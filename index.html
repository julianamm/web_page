about me

I love to learn and explore new things about JavaScript, React, HTML, CSS, SASS, Ruby on Rails and more! You can check out some of my projects.

How did I get into coding?... Well, I love learning new things and getting out of my comfort zone. My extensive experience in customer service combined with my passion for innovation, creativity and collaboration brought me naturally into the tech world. Basically, every time I would run into a customer issue, in my mind I’d be thinking “How could technology solve this problem and enhance other people’s lives???”. Now, here I am, ready for the next challenge and super committed to crafting incredible products & experiences.
